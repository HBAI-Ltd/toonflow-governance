# Toonflow Community Manifesto

> *"We believe those who build the tools should share in the value they create."*

---

## Who We Are

Toonflow is an open-source AI video production engine. Through its extensible Skills mechanism, it supports full-pipeline AI production for any video type—from short dramas and commercials to educational content. Initiated by HBAI-Ltd, it is co-built by a global community of developers.

**The Skills mechanism ensures Toonflow is not just for short dramas.** By writing Skills, developers can extend Toonflow to any video production scenario—short dramas, micro-variety shows, brand commercials, educational content, VTubers... The boundaries are defined by the community.

Since its release, Toonflow has garnered 5,000+ GitHub Stars, with a community of over 4,000 developers and creators. This isn't just traffic; it's a shared belief—a collective conviction that AI will revolutionize video production.

---

## Open-Source Boundaries: Let's Be Honest and Clear

The Toonflow core engine is permanently open-source (Apache 2.0 + Supplementary Agreement).

The following assets are privately held by HBAI-Ltd:

- **API Hub** — Providing users with out-of-the-box model access services.
- **SkillsHub** — A skill plugin marketplace where developers can publish and earn revenue shares.
- **AIGC Education Platform** — A training and education platform for AI video creation.

**Why make this clear?**
Because we believe honesty is the foundation of a community. You can only truly trust a project when you know exactly where the boundaries lie.

## Licensing Mechanism: How We Charge

**Authorization is required in only one scenario:** Packaging Toonflow as a product and selling or providing services to independent third parties (SaaS subscriptions, one-time purchases, licensed distributions, etc.).

**The following scenarios are always free and require no authorization:**

- ✅ Using Toonflow to create content, publishing it on platforms, and earning revenue shares.
- ✅ Secondary development for internal team use.
- ✅ Joint operation by ≤ 5 legal entities, for internal use only without providing external services.
- ✅ Personal learning, research, and non-commercial purposes.

## Revenue Distribution: How Licensing Fees Are Used

**HBAI-Ltd commits: 50% of all commercial licensing revenue will go directly to community development and the Community Fund.**

This is not a slogan; it is a written commitment.

### Why 50%?

HBAI-Ltd has multiple independent revenue streams (API Hub, SkillsHub revenue share, AIGC Education Platform, SaaS Commercial Edition, etc.). Commercial licensing of the Toonflow Community Edition is **not** the company's primary source of income.

This means: **We can afford to be generous.** And since we can afford it, we should do it.

### Community Fund Usage (Priorities Decided by Public Vote)

| Usage | Proportion (Ref) |
|------|------------|
| Annual Dividends for Top Contributors | ~40% |
| Community Infrastructure (Servers, Tools, Events) | ~30% |
| Community Grants (Feature Dev, Docs, Tutorials) | ~20% |
| Annual Summit / Developer Events | ~10% |

**Transparency Mechanism:**
- Quarterly publication of total licensing revenue + Community Fund allocations.
- Semi-annual publication of detailed fund usage.
- Priority voting by the Community Council (11 Contributor Seats + 4 HBAI Seats).

## Governance: A Real Voice

We established the **Toonflow Community Council**, consisting of 15 seats:

- **11 Seats** — Elected by all contributors with annual points ≥ 50, for a 1-year term, with a maximum of 2 consecutive terms.
- **4 Seats** — Appointed by HBAI-Ltd, retaining ultimate veto power.

**Rights of the Council:**
- Quarterly voting on the direction of Community Fund usage.
- Right to propose the annual revenue-sharing plan.
- Advance knowledge of the product roadmap.
- Priority access to beta testing of new features.

**Obligations of the Council:**
- Attendance at quarterly Council meetings.
- Public release of meeting summaries and voting results within 7 working days.
- Voting must prioritize the overall interests of the community; representing personal commercial interests is prohibited.
- Compliance with confidentiality obligations and the Community Code of Conduct.

**Separation of Governance and Economic Rights:**
Council seats inherently **carry no revenue-sharing eligibility**. If Council members hold personal contribution points, they participate in the revenue-sharing pool as regular developers; seats are neither weighted nor discounted.

For detailed rules, please see the [*Toonflow Community Governance Charter*](./GOVERNANCE_EN.md).

## What You Stand to Gain

### As a User
- Permanent free use of the Toonflow core engine.
- Comprehensive technical documentation and community support.

### As a Contributor
- Contribution Point System: Accumulate points through PRs, Issues, Documentation, Skill publishing, and community activity.
- Participate in the Community Fund revenue-sharing pool with annual points ≥ 50, distributed proportionally based on points.
- Points can also be redeemed for: Commercial licensing discounts and priority beta access.
- Top 30 in points with a historical cumulative ≥ 200 points are eligible to run for the Council.

### As a SkillsHub Developer
- 70/30 Revenue Split (70% to developers, 30% to the platform).
- Certified plugins are featured in official recommendation slots.
- "Powered by Toonflow" brand endorsement.

### As a Commercial Licensee
- Featured on the "Built with Toonflow" showcase page on our official website.
- Priority access to beta testing of new features.
- Join the Toonflow Partner Community.
- Half of the licensing fees you pay goes toward supporting the people building this ecosystem.

## Our Commitments

1. **The Toonflow core engine will remain permanently open-source**, regardless of how the company grows.
2. **50% of licensing revenue will be returned to the community.** This will be publicly disclosed quarterly, fully transparent, and executed from the very first transaction.
3. **We will never make the community feel competed against.** Toonflow is a tool enabler, not a content competitor.
4. **Honesty regarding boundaries.** What is private remains private; what is open-source remains open-source.
